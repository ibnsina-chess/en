About Us

THE DARK KNIGHTS IBN SINA is the official chess club of Ibn Sina High School, dedicated to fostering a passion for chess among students. Founded in 2024 by Ahmed Elyaakoubi, who also serves as our coach, the club provides a platform for students of all skill levels to come together and improve their strategic thinking, problem-solving abilities, and overall chess skills.Â  the club strives to create a supportive and competitive environment, hosting regular practice sessions, tournaments, and friendly matches. Our mission is not only to excel at the game of chess but also to build a strong sense of community, discipline, and teamwork among our members. Whether you are a beginner or an advanced player, THE DARK KNIGHTS IBN SINA welcomes you to join us and be part of a growing chess family at Ibn Sina High School.

activities

Weekly Practice Sessions
